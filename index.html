Introduction to virology

Course description

  • Time: 23h
  • Year: 2025
  • Instructor: Sopheap Oeng
  • Email: sopheap15@gmail.com
  • GitHub: sopheap15/Virology

This course introduces viruses structure, replication, pathogenesis and diagnostic methods for clinical labroratory.

Course learning outcomes

  • Distinguish viral morphology and structure
  • Explain viral multiplication and pathogenesis
  • Identify diagnostic methods used in microbiology labs
  • Describe control, prevention and treatment strategies
